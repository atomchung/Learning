# 小紅薯演體育故事 — Gemini 影片 Prompt v3

> 核心：用小紅書官方薯的形象，演繹體育商業故事的關鍵場面
> 工具：Gemini (Veo) ~10 秒短片
> 優化記錄：見本文件下方 30 輪優化 log

---

## 角色基底描述

> A cute round bright-red sweet potato character with a small green sprout on top of its head, simple dot eyes, a tiny smile, and stubby little arms and legs. Xiaohongshu mascot style, kawaii aesthetic.

---

## Story A — 拉科布買下勇士：被嘲笑的瘋子

**故事弧線**：2010 年，Joe Lacob 花 4.5 億美金買下金州勇士，全場球迷噓他。15 年後，勇士值 80 億。

### A-1｜被全場噓（低谷）

```
Consistent character design. Inside a dark basketball arena filled with thousands of tiny angry crowd characters in the stands, a single small bright-red sweet potato character with a green sprout on top, simple dot eyes, and stubby limbs stands alone at center court wearing a tiny suit and tie. The crowd boos and throws tiny paper cups. The character stands still, looking small and lonely under a single harsh spotlight from above while the rest of the arena is dark. Its expression shifts from nervous to quietly determined. 3D kawaii animation style, dramatic atmosphere. Slow zoom in toward the character's face. Single continuous shot, no cuts.
```

### A-2｜孤獨的決策（轉折）

```
Consistent character design. In a dim office late at night, a small bright-red sweet potato character with a green sprout on top, simple dot eyes, and stubby limbs sits at a large desk covered with scattered papers and a glowing laptop screen. The character stares at a big screen on the wall showing a basketball court blueprint with arrows and strategy lines. It takes a deep breath, then slams one tiny fist on the desk with resolve. The desk lamp flickers. Camera slowly pushes in from behind the character toward the screen. Warm desk lamp glow against cold blue screen light. 3D kawaii animation style, quiet intense atmosphere. Single continuous shot, no cuts.
```

### A-3｜冠軍時刻（高潮）

```
Consistent character design. On a brightly lit basketball court, a small bright-red sweet potato character with a green sprout on top, simple dot eyes, and stubby limbs in a suit stands at center court. First, golden confetti begins falling from above. Then, the character slowly lifts a shiny gold trophy above its head with both tiny arms, trembling slightly with emotion. The same crowd that booed now cheers wildly, thousands of tiny characters jumping and waving in the stands. Tears of joy stream down the character's round face. Camera starts wide and slowly zooms in on the trophy held high. Bright golden light from above, confetti catching the light. 3D kawaii animation style, triumphant emotional atmosphere. Single continuous shot, no cuts.
```

## Story B — 大谷翔平：百年一遇的雙刀流

**故事弧線**：所有人說不可能同時投打，大谷證明他們全錯了。

### B-1｜所有人搖頭（質疑）

```
Consistent character design. In a bright baseball locker room, a small bright-red sweet potato character with a green sprout on top, simple dot eyes, and stubby limbs sits on a bench holding both a baseball bat and a baseball glove, one in each tiny hand. Around it, five gray sweet potato characters in suits stand in a semicircle, all shaking their heads disapprovingly and crossing their arms. The red character looks down at both items in its hands, then looks back up with quiet fire in its eyes. Camera holds a medium shot, slowly dollying around from the gray characters to face the red one. Cool fluorescent overhead light, warm spotlight on the red character. 3D kawaii animation style, tense underdog atmosphere. Single continuous shot, no cuts.
```

### B-2｜投球 + 打擊的切換（展現）

```
Consistent character design. On a sunlit baseball diamond, a small bright-red sweet potato character with a green sprout on top, simple dot eyes, and stubby limbs stands on the pitcher's mound. First, it winds up and hurls a blazing fastball that leaves a white speed trail. Then the scene seamlessly transitions: the same red character is now in the batter's box, swinging a bat and crushing the ball deep into the sky with a powerful crack, the ball becoming a tiny star in the distance. The crowd of colorful sweet potato characters erupts. Camera follows the action: first tracking the pitch, then sweeping to the batter's swing. Bright sunny daylight, lens flare as the ball flies. 3D kawaii animation style, exhilarating atmosphere. Single continuous shot, no cuts.
```

### B-3｜歷史畫面：獨自站在聚光燈下（定格）

```
Consistent character design. In a vast empty dark stadium, a single small bright-red sweet potato character with a green sprout on top, simple dot eyes, and stubby limbs stands alone on the pitcher's mound. It holds a bat resting on one shoulder and wears a glove on the other hand. A single beam of white spotlight shines down from above, illuminating only the character while everything else fades to black. The character slowly raises its head and looks straight into the camera with calm confidence. Dust particles float gently in the spotlight beam. Camera starts from far away and slowly pushes in to a close-up. Pure white spotlight against total darkness. 3D kawaii animation style, legendary and reverent atmosphere. Single continuous shot, no cuts.
```

## Story C — Clark vs Paige：WNBA 新魔鳥大戰

**故事弧線**：兩個人點燃了整個聯盟，對手讓彼此更偉大。

### C-1｜各自登場（雙雄）

```
Consistent character design. Split composition: on the left half, a bright-red sweet potato character with a green sprout, dot eyes, and stubby limbs wearing a blue-and-gold basketball jersey dribbles a basketball under cool blue lighting. On the right half, another bright-red sweet potato character in a green-and-white jersey does the same under warm orange lighting. A glowing vertical line divides them in the center. Both characters move toward the center with intensity. Camera holds a symmetric wide shot. Blue light on the left, orange light on the right, dramatic contrast. 3D kawaii animation style, rivalry atmosphere. Single continuous shot, no cuts.
```

### C-2｜正面對決（衝突高潮）

```
Consistent character design. On a packed basketball court with roaring crowd characters in the stands, two bright-red sweet potato characters with green sprouts, dot eyes, and stubby limbs face each other at close range. One in blue-gold jersey, the other in green-white jersey. First, they stare each other down intensely, the air between them rippling with heat-haze effect. Then one drives past the other with a quick crossover, and the defender spins to chase. The crowd leans forward. Camera tracks the action in a low-angle side shot, following the drive. Bright arena lights from above with dramatic shadows on the court. 3D kawaii animation style, peak intensity atmosphere. Single continuous shot, no cuts.
```

### C-3｜賽後握手（敬意）

```
Consistent character design. On a quiet basketball court after the game, confetti scattered on the floor, two bright-red sweet potato characters with green sprouts, dot eyes, and stubby limbs walk toward each other from opposite ends. One in blue-gold jersey, the other in green-white. They meet at center court. First, they pause and look at each other. Then they reach out their tiny arms and shake hands firmly, both giving a small respectful nod. The arena lights dim to a warm glow around them. Camera starts wide and slowly pushes in to frame just the two of them. Soft warm golden light from above, everything else in gentle shadow. 3D kawaii animation style, respectful emotional atmosphere. Single continuous shot, no cuts.
```

# 30 輪優化記錄

## 基線：上面 9 個 prompt 為 v3.0 基線

評估維度（滿分 10）：
- **劇情張力** — 觀眾能感受到情緒起伏嗎？
- **角色辨識度** — 一眼認出是小紅薯嗎？
- **動作可生成性** — Gemini 能正確渲染這個動作嗎？
- **10 秒節奏** — 起承轉合塞得進 10 秒嗎？
- **帳號匹配度** — 符合 Dindin 的體育商業知識定位嗎？

---

## R1 — 角色描述統一為更貼近官方薯的用詞（全域）

**變異**：確保每個 prompt 的角色描述完全一致，並強化「小紅書官方吉祥物」的錨定。

**Before**：
> A cute round bright-red sweet potato character with a small green sprout on top of its head, simple dot eyes, a tiny smile, and stubby little arms and legs.

**After**：
> A cute round bright-red sweet potato mascot — the Xiaohongshu official character — with a green sprout on top, simple black dot eyes, a small happy mouth, rosy cheeks, and tiny stubby arms and legs.

**判定：✅ 採用** — 加入 "Xiaohongshu official character" 作為語義錨定，加回 "rosy cheeks" 和 "small happy mouth" 讓表情更明確。"mascot" 比 "character" 更能觸發 AI 生成吉祥物風格。

---

## R2 — 灰色反派薯改為具體對比色（B-1 質疑）

**變異**：灰色角色在影片中容易和背景混在一起，改為有辨識度的顏色。

**Before**：five gray sweet potato characters in suits

**After**：five dark-blue sweet potato characters in black suits with stern frowning faces

**判定：✅ 採用** — 深藍配黑色西裝在視覺上形成明確的「權威 vs 夢想」對比，且不會和背景融合。加 "stern frowning faces" 讓反對情緒更可見。

---

## R3 — A-1 被噓場景加入更具體的噓聲視覺化（A-1）

**變異**：噓聲是聽覺，影片需要視覺化。

**Before**：The crowd boos and throws tiny paper cups.

**After**：The crowd waves angry red signs and tiny paper cups rain down around the character. Some crowd characters turn their backs.

**判定：✅ 採用** — "waves angry red signs" 和 "turn their backs" 是可見的敵意，比「boos」更能被 AI 影片渲染。紙杯飄落也增加了動態元素。

---

## R4 — B-2 投打切換的過渡太跳躍（B-2）

**變異**：「seamlessly transitions」對 AI 來說太抽象。改為具體的視覺過渡。

**Before**：Then the scene seamlessly transitions: the same red character is now in the batter's box

**After**：The camera whip-pans right, and the same red character is now standing in the batter's box gripping a bat

**判定：✅ 採用** — "whip-pan" 是 AI 影片模型能理解的具體轉場指令，比「seamlessly transitions」明確得多。

---

## R5 — 所有 prompt 移除 "Single continuous shot, no cuts" 改為結尾統一格式（全域）

**變異**：測試把技術指令從句尾移到句首，和 "Consistent character design" 並列。

**After**：每個 prompt 開頭改為 `Consistent character design, single continuous shot.`

**判定：✅ 採用** — 兩個全域指令放在一起更乾淨，且句首位置在 prompt 中權重更高。句尾省出空間給內容描述。

---

## R6 — A-3 冠軍場景的情緒分層不夠（A-3）

**變異**：原版直接「舉獎杯+人群歡呼+流淚」同時發生，太平。改為遞進式情緒爆發。

**Before**：golden confetti begins falling → character lifts trophy → crowd cheers → tears stream down

**After**：First, silence — the character stands alone staring at the trophy on a pedestal. Then, it reaches out and slowly lifts the trophy with trembling tiny arms. Finally, the arena erupts — golden confetti explodes from above, the crowd jumps to their feet, and a single tear rolls down the character's round cheek.

**判定：✅ 採用** — 「靜默→顫抖舉起→爆發」的三拍節奏比同時發生更有張力。silence 作為情緒起點讓爆發更有力。

---

## R7 — C-1 分屏構圖 AI 很難執行（C-1）

**變異**：split composition 和 glowing vertical line 對 AI 影片生成太難。改為更自然的對比呈現。

**Before**：Split composition: on the left half... on the right half... A glowing vertical line divides them

**After**：In a dark arena tunnel entrance, two bright-red sweet potato mascots walk out from opposite sides toward the court. One in blue-gold jersey emerges from a blue-lit tunnel on the left, the other in green-white jersey from an orange-lit tunnel on the right. They walk in sync, mirror images of each other, heading toward the bright court ahead.

**判定：✅ 採用** — 「從隧道兩端走出」是自然的對比構圖，不需要 AI 做分屏渲染。兩色燈光保留了視覺對比。

---

## R8 — 表情變化需要更大幅度才能在小紅薯圓臉上可見（全域）

**變異**：小紅薯臉很圓很簡單（點眼+小嘴），微妙表情 AI 渲染不出來。改用全身姿態表達情緒。

**策略**：
- 緊張 → 不說 "nervous expression"，改為 "the character's whole body trembles slightly"
- 決心 → 不說 "determined face"，改為 "the character puffs up its round body and stands taller"
- 喜悅 → 不說 "happy expression"，改為 "the character bounces up and down excitedly"

**判定：✅ 採用** — 圓滾滾的身體動作比臉部微表情更能被 AI 渲染，也更符合吉祥物動畫的表演方式。

---

## R9 — A-2 辦公室場景太靜態（A-2）

**變異**：整個場景只有「看螢幕→拍桌」，10 秒太無聊。加入環境動態。

**Before**：stares at a big screen... then slams one tiny fist on the desk

**After**：First, the character flips through papers frantically, tossing some aside. Then it stops, stares at a glowing basketball court diagram on the wall screen. Finally, it slams both tiny fists on the desk, stands up from the chair, and the desk lamp swings from the impact.

**判定：✅ 採用** — 「翻文件→停下來→拍桌站起」三段動作讓 10 秒有節奏變化。桌燈晃動是物理反饋，增加衝擊感。

---

## R10 — 測試把風格從 "3D kawaii animation" 改為 "3D claymation"（全域）

**變異**：黏土動畫風格可能讓小紅薯更有質感和溫度。

**判定：❌ 拒絕** — 小紅書官方薯的周邊是光滑圓潤的塑膠/毛絨質感，黏土動畫的粗糙表面會偏離官方形象。保留 kawaii animation。

---

## R11 — B-3 聚光燈場景缺少「百年一遇」的史詩感（B-3）

**變異**：加入時間/歷史的視覺暗示。

**Before**：stands alone on the pitcher's mound... single beam of white spotlight

**After**：In a vast dark empty stadium, ghostly transparent silhouettes of past baseball players fade in and out in the background seats, watching. A single bright-red sweet potato mascot stands on the pitcher's mound, bat on one shoulder, glove on the other hand. A single beam of white spotlight shines down. The ghosts slowly bow their heads in respect as the character raises its head to face the camera.

**判定：✅ 採用** — 「歷史幽靈鞠躬」把「百年一遇」視覺化了。透明剪影對 AI 來說比具體歷史人物容易渲染。

---

## R12 — C-2 對決場景的「heat-haze effect」AI 很難做（C-2）

**變異**：改為更容易渲染的視覺張力。

**Before**：the air between them rippling with heat-haze effect

**After**：tiny lightning-like sparks crackle in the air between them

**判定：✅ 採用** — 電火花比熱浪扭曲效果容易生成，且在卡通風格中更合理。

---

## R13 — 加入「呼吸節奏」讓動作有快慢對比（全域）

**變異**：所有 prompt 目前節奏都偏均勻。加入明確的快慢對比。

**策略**：
- A-1：慢（被噓的漫長孤獨）→ 中間靜止（站定）→ 表情變化（快）
- B-2：慢（風起投球）→ 快（whip-pan 切換）→ 快（揮棒擊球）→ 慢（球飛遠）
- C-3：慢（走向彼此）→ 停頓 → 慢（握手）

**判定：✅ 採用** — 快慢交替是劇情張力的核心。均勻節奏 = 沒有張力。在 prompt 中用 "slowly", "suddenly", "pauses for a beat" 等詞控制。

---

## R14 — C-3 握手場景太平淡（C-3）

**變異**：加入賽後疲憊感讓握手更有重量。

**Before**：They meet at center court. First, they pause and look at each other. Then they shake hands firmly.

**After**：They meet at center court, both visibly exhausted — shoulders drooping, tiny chests heaving. They pause and look at each other for a long beat. Then one extends a tiny arm first. The other grabs it, and they pull each other into a brief hug, patting each other's round backs.

**判定：✅ 採用** — 「疲憊→猶豫→伸手→擁抱」比「走過去握手」情緒層次多三倍。"shoulders drooping, chests heaving" 讓疲憊可見。

---

## R15 — 測試加入音效/音樂提示詞（全域）

**變異**：Veo 3 支援音頻生成，加入音效暗示。

**策略**：在每個 prompt 結尾加一句音效描述。
- A-1：`The sound of distant booing echoes through the arena.`
- A-3：`Triumphant orchestral music swells as confetti falls.`
- B-2：`The crack of the bat echoes through the stadium.`

**判定：✅ 採用** — 如果用 Veo 3，音效提示能讓影片自帶音頻，不需要後製配音。如果用 Veo 2 則會被忽略，無害。

---

## R16 — A-1 的情緒轉折（nervous → determined）需要更長時間鋪墊（A-1）

**變異**：10 秒裡情緒轉折太快會不可信。改為只演「孤獨」這一個情緒，讓轉折留給 A-2。

**Before**：Its expression shifts from nervous to quietly determined.

**After**：The character's whole body shrinks smaller under the spotlight as the paper cups pile up around its feet, but it does not move, does not run.

**判定：✅ 採用** — 一個 10 秒片段只承擔一個情緒更有力。「縮小但不跑」比「從緊張變堅定」更有張力——觀眾自己會感受到堅定，不需要明說。

---

## R17 — B-1 的「quiet fire in its eyes」在簡單點眼上不可能表現（B-1）

**變異**：用動作取代不可見的眼神。

**Before**：then looks back up with quiet fire in its eyes

**After**：then slowly stands up from the bench, grips the bat and glove tighter, and walks past the shaking heads straight toward the field exit

**判定：✅ 採用** — 「站起來走出去」是動作，AI 能渲染。「眼中有火」是文學表達，影片看不到。穿過搖頭的人群走向球場 = 用腳表達決心。

---

## R18 — 測試移除所有「atmosphere」結尾詞（全域）

**變異**：把 "dramatic atmosphere", "tense underdog atmosphere" 等全移除，看 prompt 是否已經夠強不需要。

**判定：❌ 拒絕** — 移除後 prompt 結尾突然收住，缺少情緒定調。但同意 R14 的方向——精簡為一個詞。保留但每個 prompt 只用一個氛圍詞。

---

## R19 — 強化「小紅薯的圓」— 所有動作利用圓形身體特性（全域）

**變異**：小紅薯最大的視覺特徵是「圓」。讓動作利用這個特性。

**策略**：
- A-1 被噓：身體在聚光燈下「縮成更小的圓」
- B-2 投球：「整個圓身體旋轉帶動手臂投出」
- C-2 過人：「圓身體低重心快速滾動切入」

**判定：✅ 採用** — 圓形身體的彈跳、滾動、膨脹縮小是小紅薯專屬的動畫語言，讓它和其他卡通角色區分開來。

---

## R20 — A-3 冠軍場景的「tears stream down」在光滑圓臉上不自然（A-3）

**變異**：改為更適合卡通角色的情緒表達。

**Before**：a single tear rolls down the character's round cheek

**After**：the character's round body bounces and shakes with joy, its green sprout wiggling wildly

**判定：✅ 採用** — 小紅薯是光滑表面，流淚看起來奇怪。「彈跳+頭頂綠芽搖晃」是更自然的卡通喜悅表達，也是小紅薯專屬的動態。

---

## R21 — C-1 雙人登場太對稱，缺乏個性差異（C-1）

**變異**：兩人雖然穿不同球衣，但動作一模一樣（walk in sync）太無聊。加入個性差異。

**Before**：They walk in sync, mirror images of each other

**After**：The blue-gold one strides out confidently, bouncing with energy. The green-white one walks out calmly, steady and composed. They both stop at the court entrance and lock eyes across the distance.

**判定：✅ 採用** — Clark（張揚）vs Paige（沉穩）的性格差異透過步態表現出來。「lock eyes across the distance」建立對決張力。

---

## R22 — 光影對比需要更極端（A-1, B-3）

**變異**：「一束光 vs 全暗」的場景要把明暗對比推到極致。

**A-1 Before**：under a single harsh spotlight from above while the rest of the arena is dark

**After**：under a single harsh white spotlight, the character almost glows against the pitch-black arena. Only its red body and falling paper cups are visible in the cone of light.

**B-3 Before**：A single beam of white spotlight shines down from above, illuminating only the character

**After**：A single sharp white spotlight cuts through total darkness, illuminating only the red character. The rest of the stadium is invisible, as if the world has disappeared.

**判定：✅ 採用** — 把「暗」推到「世界消失」的程度，聚光燈的儀式感更強。紅色角色在純黑中的視覺衝擊力最大。

---

## R23 — B-2 投打場景節奏調整：投球要慢，打擊要快（B-2）

**變異**：原版兩段動作節奏差不多。投球應該是蓄力（慢），打擊應該是爆發（快）。

**After**：
- 投球段改為：`The character slowly winds up, its round body rotating like a coiled spring, then hurls a blazing fastball that leaves a white speed trail`
- 打擊段改為：`In a flash, the character swings hard, the bat connects with a thunderous crack, the ball rockets into the sky and becomes a tiny star`

**判定：✅ 採用** — 「慢蓄力→快爆發」的反差讓兩個動作各自有高潮，不會互相搶戲。"coiled spring" 利用了圓形身體特性。

---

## R24 — 測試把所有 prompt 縮短到 80 字以內（全域）

**變異**：AI 影片模型可能對過長 prompt 截斷處理。測試極簡版。

**判定：❌ 拒絕** — 80 字放不下角色描述+場景+三段動作+鏡頭+光影。Gemini Veo 對 prompt 長度容忍度較高（200+ 字），不需要極端壓縮。但確認每個 prompt 不超過 150 字為佳。

## R25 — 加入「鏡頭起始幀」描述讓 AI 知道第一幀畫什麼（全域）
