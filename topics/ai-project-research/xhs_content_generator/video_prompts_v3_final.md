# Story A — 拉科布買下勇士：被嘲笑的瘋子

## A-1｜被全場噓（低谷）

```
Consistent character design, single continuous shot. The shot opens on an empty spotlight circle on a dark court floor. In a pitch-black basketball arena, a single small bright-red sweet potato mascot with a green sprout on top, dot eyes, rosy cheeks, and stubby limbs, soft plush toy texture, wearing a tiny suit, stands alone at center court. A harsh white spotlight makes its red body glow against total darkness. Crowds of tiny characters in the dark stands wave angry signs as paper cups rain down slowly around it. The character's whole body shrinks smaller and smaller under the pressure, but it does not move, does not run. Only its red body and falling cups are visible in the cone of light. Slow zoom in toward the character. The sound of distant booing echoes through the arena. 3D Pixar-style kawaii animation, heavy atmosphere.
```

## A-2｜孤獨的決策（轉折）

# Story B — 大谷翔平：百年一遇的雙刀流

## B-1｜所有人搖頭（質疑）

```
Consistent character design, single continuous shot. The shot opens on a bright baseball locker room. A small bright-red sweet potato mascot with a green sprout on top, dot eyes, rosy cheeks, and stubby limbs, soft plush toy texture, sits on a bench holding a baseball bat in one hand and a glove in the other. Around it, five dark-blue sweet potato characters in black suits stand in a semicircle, all shaking their heads with stern frowning faces and crossed arms. The red character pauses, then slowly stands up from the bench, grips the bat and glove tighter, and walks past the shaking heads straight toward the field exit. Camera slowly dollies around from the blue characters to face the red one walking away. Cool fluorescent light above, warm glow from the field exit ahead. 3D Pixar-style kawaii animation, underdog atmosphere.
```

## B-2｜投球 + 打擊（展現）

```
Consistent character design, single continuous shot. First, it slowly winds up, its round body rotating like a coiled spring, then hurls a blazing fastball that leaves a white speed trail. The camera whip-pans right — the same red character now stands in the batter's box gripping a bat. In a flash, it swings hard, its whole round body spinning with the force, the bat connects with a thunderous crack, and the ball rockets into the sky becoming a tiny star. Colorful sweet potato crowds erupt. Bright sunny daylight, lens flare as the ball flies. The crack of the bat echoes. 3D Pixar-style kawaii animation, exhilarating atmosphere.

## 使用指南

### 每個 Story 的 3 支影片用途

| 段落 | 情緒 | 小紅書用途 |
|------|------|-----------|
| X-1 | 低谷/起點 | 鉤子影片，吸引點擊 |
| X-2 | 轉折/行動 | 正文搭配，講解商業邏輯 |
| X-3 | 高潮/收尾 | 情緒收尾，引導互動 |

### 搭配圖文筆記

每支影片可搭配一篇你既有的圖文內容：
- Story A → 「💙💛NBA金州勇士主理人，15年賺20倍｜拉科布」
- Story B → 大谷翔平相關內容
- Story C → 「🏀 WNBA 魔鳥大戰｜克拉克VS佩奇誰更強？」

### Gemini 設定建議
- 時長：選 10 秒
- 比例：16:9（橫版）或 9:16（豎版，更適合小紅書 feed）
- 每個 prompt 跑 3-5 次，挑最佳版本
- 如果角色偏移，嘗試先用圖片生成一張小紅薯角色圖，再用 image-to-video 模式
